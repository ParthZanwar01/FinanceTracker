Financial Tips & Tricks

50/30/20 Rule

Allocate 50% of your income to needs, 30% to wants, and 20% to savings and debt repayment. This simple budget can help you maintain financial balance.

Emergency Fund

Aim to save 3-6 months of living expenses in an easily accessible account. This provides financial security in case of unexpected events.

Debt Snowball Method

Pay minimum payments on all debts, then focus extra money on your smallest debt first. Once paid off, roll that payment into the next smallest debt.

Pay Yourself First

Set up automatic transfers to your savings account on payday before spending on other things. This ensures consistent saving habits.
